The Future of IWantToWorkAtEA.com

Wed 01 October 2003 #IWantToWorkAtEA.com

I was thinking the other day about what should happen to this domain once I land a gig at EA. What Id like to do is find someone whose trying to get a gig at EA like I was and hand the steering wheel off to them so they can add their experiences. I’ll contribute advice and insight into my experience from the inside as well as help this person along. The domain would become a repository for the stories of people trying to get into the industry (and EA specifically), passing from one aspiring game person to the next. Just like the Dread Pirate Roberts.
