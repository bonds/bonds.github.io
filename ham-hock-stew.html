Ham Hock Stew

Mon 18 July 2022 #Food #Recipe

Makes 8 servings.

  • 1 ham hock
  • 16 ounces (1 package) black eye peas
  • 4 cups white rice

Defart the Beans

Before cooking the beans, soak overnight in water. Throw out water. Repeat.

Make the Stew

Add ham hock to a large pot. Add black eye peas. Fill pot with water until ham hock is covered. Simmer for 4 hours. Make rice. Serve stew over rice.
